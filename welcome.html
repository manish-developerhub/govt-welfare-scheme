Major Indian Government Welfare Schemes

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

Provides direct income support of ₹6000/year to all landholding farmer families.

  • Amount transferred in 3 equal installments.
  • Helps in agricultural and household expenses.
  • Online registration & transparent DBT process.

PMJAY (Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana)

Offers health insurance cover up to ₹5 lakh/family/year for secondary & tertiary care.

  • Free treatment at empanelled hospitals.
  • Over 10 crore families covered.
  • Cashless & paperless hospitalization.

MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)

Guarantees 100 days of wage employment to rural households.

  • Empowers rural poor by providing job security.
  • Women participation encouraged.
  • Improves infrastructure in villages.

Ujjwala Yojana

Provides free LPG connections to BPL families to promote clean cooking.

  • Over 8 crore families benefitted.
  • Reduces indoor air pollution.
  • Empowers women with better health and convenience.

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

To provide skill training to youth for better employment and self-employment opportunities.

  • To train youth in skills that are actually needed in the job market.
  • To make young people employable or help them start their own work.
  • To create a skilled workforce that supports India's economic growth.
